About me

ANDREW BOYER

U.S. Air Force Veteran

Spent 6 years as an Aircraft Hydraulics Systems Technician working on hydraulic componenents for the B-52 Stratofortress, B-1 Lancer and B-2 Spirit nuclear bombers.

Space Enthusiast

Fascination with space and the mysteries that await for us there. I grew up with looking through a telescope, piloting some spacecraft in a game, or watching rocket launches like Falcon 9 and Starship. I would love to be on board on of those rockets one day and see Earth in a way that so little people have.

Coder

Mobile and web application development, serverless testing utilizing Amazon Web Services and Nordstrom’s serverless artillery project, built/compiled/fitted models for machine learning, and created and managed SQL databases.

Wildlife Photographer

I love the outdoors and wildlife photography gives me a purpose to go out there and explore everything nature has to offer. My goal with photography is to get ultra-high quality photos of as many species as possible in their natural world and use those images to populate a project of mine: www.pnwwildlife.com.
